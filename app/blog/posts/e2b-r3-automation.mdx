# E2B(R3) XML Automation: Generate ICH-Compliant Safety Reports in 20ms

ICH E2B(R3) submissions are one of the most time-consuming regulatory requirements in pharmacovigilance. Each Individual Case Safety Report (ICSR) requires precise XML formatting across 200+ data elements — and errors trigger regulatory rejection.

## What Is E2B(R3)?

ICH E2B(R3) is the international standard for electronic transmission of individual case safety reports (ICSRs) to regulatory authorities including FDA, EMA, PMDA, and Health Canada. 

Key requirements:
- **15-day expedited reporting** for serious unexpected adverse drug reactions (SUSARs)
- **90-day routine reporting** for non-serious or expected reactions
- Strict XML schema validation against ICH E2B(R3) DTD

## The Manual E2B Challenge

Manual E2B(R3) report generation typically requires:
- 8 hours per expedited (15-day) report
- Expert knowledge of 200+ E2B data elements
- Manual MedDRA coding for each adverse event term
- XML validation and error correction

At $85/hour, that's **$680 per expedited report** — and most organizations process 250+ expedited reports per year.
